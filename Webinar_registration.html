DG / HAZMAT HAULING SOLUTION FOR 3PL

Safe transportation of Dangerous Goods relies on using the prescribed packaging and limiting the quantities for the mode of transport. Compliance with the regulations is reflected in the Dangerous Goods Declaration which tells handlers what danger is being transported and it is packaged as per the regulations. DGDox™ is a cloud solution – executing on a browser where ever the internet is available Scales for small to large 3PLs and shippers. Allows 3PL’s to empower customers with their private sub-portals. Seeing is believing – Attend our webinar to see it in action.

Session: October 19th 2021, 3:00 PM CET

The webinar will demonstrate:

  • Select the correct packaging and labeling and leverage Special Provisions for the selected UN Number
  • Generate compliant DG Declarations for ADR, IATA, IMDG, TDG, 49CFR
  • Segregate shipments as per regulations
  • Generate Shipping Labels for FedEx Ground
  • How to create Overpack shipment and generate Overpack labels
  • All packed in one mode for IATA shipments
  • Segregation as per regulations
